Christ the Redemeer is an Art Deco statue. It is located in Mount Corcovado in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It stands at 30 metres (98 ft) high. The statue is occasionally struck by lightning due to its height and location on the mountain. Christ the Redeemer has been deemed now as a new 7 wonders of the world.

Escadaria Selaron was created by Jorge Selarón, a Chilean-born artist who moved to Rio de Janeiro in the 1980s. The Escadaria Selarón is a set of 215 steps that connect the neighborhoods of Santa Teresa and Lapa, in the heart of Rio de Janeiro. The steps are decorated with over 2000 brightly colored tiles, which include pieces from over 60 countries. he steps gained worldwide fame and became a must-visit attraction for tourists visiting Rio.

The Amazon Theatre is located at the heart of brazil. The Amazon Theatre was inaugurated on December 31, 1896, during a period of great economic prosperity in Manaus, fueled by the rubber boom. The theatre was built to reflect the wealth and importance of the region at the time, symbolizing the city’s growing cultural influence and international aspirations. The Amazon Theatre is one of Manaus' most visited tourist attractions, drawing thousands of visitors each year who come to admire its historical and architectural beauty.

The Iguaza Falls Is located in parts of brazil and argentina. It is one of the most impressive natural wonders of the world, is located on the border between Argentina and Brazil. Here are some fascinating facts about this breathtaking waterfall. Iguazú Falls is made up of about 275 individual waterfalls spread over nearly 2 miles (around 3 kilometers) of the Iguazú River.
